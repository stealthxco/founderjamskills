# Attribution

The Founder Jam Skills Pack references frameworks and methodologies from the following published sources. All frameworks are used for educational purposes within the workshop context.

## Strategy Frameworks

**Good Strategy / Bad Strategy**
Richard Rumelt (Crown Business, 2011)
The Kernel of Good Strategy: Diagnosis (what's going on), Guiding Policy (the approach), Coherent Action (the coordinated set of actions). Used in battle-card-builder and hypothesis-engine to structure competitive analysis and strategic hypothesis development.

**The Four Steps to the Epiphany**
Steve Blank (K&S Ranch, 2013)
Customer Development methodology: Customer Discovery, Customer Validation, Customer Creation, Company Building. Used throughout the skills pack to frame the validation-first approach to startup building.

## Customer & Market Frameworks

**Competing Against Luck / Jobs-to-be-Done**
Clayton Christensen (Harper Business, 2016)
JTBD framework: "When [trigger event], I want to [motivation/job], so I can [desired outcome]." Used in icp-definer and offer-designer to define ideal customer profiles through the lens of the job the customer is trying to accomplish.

**Value Proposition Design**
Alexander Osterwalder, Yves Pigneur, Greg Bernarda, Alan Smith (Wiley, 2014)
Value Proposition Canvas: Customer Profile (jobs, pains, gains) mapped to Value Map (products, pain relievers, gain creators). Used as an optional pre-step in offer-designer to validate positioning before lead magnet design.

**Crossing the Chasm / Beachhead Market**
Geoffrey Moore (HarperBusiness, 1991); Bill Aulet, *Disciplined Entrepreneurship* (Wiley, 2013)
Beachhead market selection: pick one narrow, winnable segment to dominate before expanding. Used in icp-definer to guide segment selection and avoid the "we can help anyone" trap.

## Design Methodology

**Double Diamond**
Design Council UK (public domain)
Four-phase design process: Discover (diverge), Define (converge), Develop (diverge), Deliver (converge). Used in hypothesis-engine to structure the diverge-then-converge approach to hypothesis generation and selection.

## Lead Magnet & Offer Frameworks

**$100M Offers / $100M Leads**
Alex Hormozi (Acquisition.com, 2021 / 2023)
Lead Magnet Taxonomy (Problem Revealing, Samples & Trials, One Step of Multi-Step), MAGIC Naming Formula, Value Equation (Dream Outcome x Perceived Likelihood / Time Delay x Effort & Sacrifice), Grand Slam Offer creation process. Used in lead-magnet-ideator for concept generation and scoring, and referenced in offer-designer for lead magnet design principles.

**Hyper-Congruent Lead Magnets / Free Micro-Tools as Lead Magnets**
Greg Isenberg (Late Checkout / Startup Ideas Podcast, 2024-2025)
Content-specific lead magnets matched to individual pieces of content, micro-tool lead magnets over static PDFs, validation cascade methodology (test concept as social post before building). Used in lead-magnet-ideator for the validation step and micro-tool concept generation.

**AI-Powered Marketing Asset Creation**
James Dickerson (The Vibe Marketer / BoringMarketing, 2024-2025)
AI-first asset creation methodology (80% AI generation, 20% human insight), lead magnet velocity and rotation principles, value-over-vanity approach to lead magnets. Used in lead-magnet-ideator for build estimation and concept diversity guidance.

## Growth Operations Frameworks

**89% Show-Rate Protocol / Lead Nurture Playbook**
Alex Hormozi (Acquisition.com / $100M Leads, 2023)
Data-driven confirmation sequence achieving up to 89% show rates: immediate confirmation with proof, 24-hour reminder with agenda, 1-hour reminder with value note. Pre-call value note with Proof-Promise-Plan opening. No-show recovery cadence. Used in show-rate-optimizer.

**Hook-Retain-Reward / Content-to-Conversion**
Alex Hormozi (Acquisition.com, 2021-2025)
Hook (capture attention), Retain (maintain engagement through stories and insights), Reward (genuine value and actionable takeaways). Give-to-ask ratios (3.5:1 minimum). Used in case-study-generator for blog post structure and content-calendar-planner for ratio enforcement.

**Grand Slam Offer (9-Step Process) / Bonus Stacking / Guarantee Design**
Alex Hormozi ($100M Offers, 2021)
9-step process: Find Starving Crowd, Map Obstacles, Create Solutions, Apply Value Equation, Trim and Stack, MAGIC Naming, Design Guarantee (4 tiers), Add Ethical Scarcity, Design Upsell/Downsell Architecture. Used in offer-architect for advanced package optimization.

**Essential Page Elements / Landing Page Conversion**
Alex Hormozi (Acquisition.com, 2021-2025)
Offer strength over page design. Essential elements: headline (third-grade reading level), social proof near CTAs, "stupid simple" directions, FAQ addressing objections, weekly A/B testing protocol. Used in landing-page-auditor for the 8-dimension audit scorecard.

**7-Email Welcome Sequence Framework**
James Dickerson (The Vibe Marketer / BoringMarketing, 2024-2025)
Deliver-Connect-Value-Value-Bridge-Soft Pitch-Direct structure. Source-specific sequences for assessment completers, lead magnet downloaders, contact form submissions, and newsletter subscribers. Used in nurture-engine.

**6 Circles Method for Keyword Research**
James Dickerson (The Vibe Marketer, 2024-2025)
Six circles of keyword opportunity: What You Sell, Problems You Solve, Outcomes You Deliver, Unique Positioning, Adjacent Topics, Entities to Associate. Programmatic SEO templates for scalable page creation. Publishing velocity rules. Used in seo-keyword-mapper.

**Content Atomizer System / Traffic Flywheel**
James Dickerson (The Vibe Marketer / BoringMarketing, 2024-2025)
One core piece becomes 15+ derivative assets across platforms. Rejection cycle process for quality gates. Human checkpoint checklist (non-negotiable before publishing). Used in content-calendar-planner for cadence and velocity guidance.

**Positioning Angle Templates / Rejection Cycles**
James Dickerson (The Vibe Marketer, 2024-2025)
Direct response positioning templates by business model (Specialist, Proprietary Methodology, Results Track Record, Anti-Agency). Expert review checkpoint protocol with multi-perspective validation. Used in landing-page-auditor.

**Champion Activation Strategy / Community Advocacy**
Greg Isenberg (Late Checkout / Startup Ideas Podcast, 2024-2025)
Champion identification (Enthusiasm x Results x Network scoring), activation actions (early access, events, specific referral requests, content collaboration), three-way introduction template. Used in testimonial-collector.

**Validation Cascade / Content Triplets**
Greg Isenberg (Late Checkout, 2024-2025)
Four-stage validation: one-liner post (test), full post (expand), blog post (develop), amplify (scale). Content Triplets: one idea becomes thread + blog + video minimum. Used in seo-keyword-mapper and content-calendar-planner for topic validation.

**Hyper-Congruent Landing Pages / Persona-Specific Microsites**
Greg Isenberg (Late Checkout / Startup Ideas Podcast, 2024-2025)
Each landing page specifically tailored to match the traffic source ("coherent with the content people are coming from"). Persona-specific microsites at minimal cost. Used in landing-page-auditor for the congruence dimension.

**Proof Playbook / Objection-to-Testimonial Pattern**
Alex Hormozi (Acquisition.com / $100M Leads, 2023)
Strongest testimonials counter specific buying objections. Map each common objection to a testimonial question designed to elicit proof against that doubt. Social proof placement strategy: testimonials near CTAs, matching proof type to the doubt at each decision point. Used in testimonial-collector.

**Referral Flywheel**
James Dickerson (The Vibe Marketer, 2024-2025)
Deliver results -> Document as case study -> Publish as content -> Build reputation -> Generate referrals -> Deliver more results (repeat). Every case study should accelerate the flywheel. Used in case-study-generator.
