# Facilitator Provocative Questions

**Used in:** All skills (facilitated_mode: true)
**Purpose:** Challenge founders to go deeper, be specific, and avoid comfortable assumptions

These questions are embedded in each skill when `facilitated_mode` is enabled. Use them to push founders past surface-level answers toward the insight that actually changes their direction.

---

## founder-setup

*Used during: Pre-workshop kickoff / problem-solution fit scoring*

- "Is the problem painful enough that someone would pay today to solve it?"
- "Who has this problem the worst? Are you building for them?"
- "What have you already tried to validate this?"

---

## pitch-builder

*Used during: End-of-day share-out preparation*

- "If you only had 60 seconds, what's the one thing you want people to remember?"
- "What's the ask that would actually move you forward this week?"
- "What did you learn that surprised you most?"

---

## validation-planner

*Used during: End-of-day commitment planning*

- "What's the smallest experiment you could run in 48 hours that would tell you something real?"
- "If you hit Yellow in 30 days, what's your decision rule for pivoting vs. optimizing?"
- "Who outside this room will hold you accountable to these numbers?"

---

## battle-card-builder

*Used during: Product Jam - Competitive Landscape activity*

- "What job is the customer hiring today without you?"
- "Where are switching costs highest -- and is that a moat or a trap?"
- "What's your unfair advantage the big players can't copy in 18 months?"
- "Which competitor are you most scared of? Why?"
- "If you lost to a competitor today, which one would it be -- and what would they have done right?"

---

## hypothesis-engine

*Used during: Product Jam - Hypothesis Design activity*

- "Which hypothesis are you avoiding because it would require the most change? That's probably the most important one."
- "What's the fastest you could know if this hypothesis is wrong?"
- "Are you testing a real risk to the business, or confirming something you already believe?"
- "What would it take to prove this wrong in 2 weeks?"

---

## prototype-launcher

*Used during: Product Jam - Rapid Prototyping activity*

- "What's the riskiest assumption in your prototype? Can you test that first?"
- "Who can you show this to in the next 24 hours?"
- "What would make you confident enough to put money behind this?"
- "If this prototype fails, what will you have learned?"

---

## icp-definer

*Used during: Growth Jam - ICP + Distribution Strategy*

- "Who has this problem the worst, right now, and would pay the most to fix it?"
- "Which competitor is growing fastest -- and what are they doing that you could do better?"
- "Where does your ICP already go to solve adjacent problems?"
- "What channel gives you 100 conversations in 30 days without paid budget?"
- "If you picked the wrong beachhead, how long until you'd know?"

---

## offer-designer

*Used during: Growth Jam - Offer + Lead Magnet*

- "Would you give your email address for this lead magnet? Would your best customer?"
- "What would make someone forward this email to a colleague?"
- "Which email in the sequence is most likely to get a reply? Is it soon enough?"
- "What's the one outcome you want from this sequence -- a reply, a booking, a purchase?"

---

## growth-builder

*Used during: Growth Jam - Automation + Metrics + 30-Day Plan*

- "Which automation will save you the most time in the next 30 days?"
- "If you could only track one number for 90 days, what would tell you if this is working?"
- "What's your decision rule for knowing when to pivot vs. persist?"
- "Who will review these metrics with you in 30 days?"

---

## market-analyzer

*Used during: On-demand market research (both tracks)*

- "Is the market big enough to build a venture-scale business, or should this be a lifestyle business? Both are valid."
- "Which competitor is winning -- and what does that tell you about what the market values?"
- "What does the market size tell you about your pricing ceiling?"
- "If a well-funded competitor entered your space tomorrow, where would you be most vulnerable?"
