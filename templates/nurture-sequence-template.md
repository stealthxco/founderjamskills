# Nurture Sequence Template

**Used in:** offer-designer skill
**Format:** Miro-ready -- paste as a sequential sticky note flow

A 7-email sequence that moves a new lead from awareness to readiness over 21 days. Each email has one job. Do not try to do two things in one email.

---

## Sequence Overview

| Email | Day | Job | CTA Type |
|-------|-----|-----|----------|
| Email 1 | Day 0 | Deliver + Welcome | Set expectations |
| Email 2 | Day 2 | Pure Value | No ask |
| Email 3 | Day 5 | Education | Soft engagement |
| Email 4 | Day 8 | Social Proof | Build trust |
| Email 5 | Day 12 | Value + Soft CTA | First offer |
| Email 6 | Day 16 | Address Objection | Remove friction |
| Email 7 | Day 21 | Ongoing Invitation | Long-term engagement |

---

## Email 1 -- Day 0: Deliver + Welcome

**Job:** Deliver the lead magnet immediately. Set expectations for what's coming. Make them feel like joining was the right call.

| Field | Your Draft |
|-------|------------|
| **Subject Line** | |
| **Opening** | Deliver the lead magnet or resource they signed up for |
| **Body** | 2-3 sentences: who you are, what you help people do, what they can expect from this sequence |
| **CTA** | Tell them what's coming next (no ask yet) |

---

## Email 2 -- Day 2: Pure Value

**Job:** Give something useful with zero strings attached. Build credibility. No pitch, no offer.

| Field | Your Draft |
|-------|------------|
| **Subject Line** | |
| **Opening** | One insight, tip, or framework relevant to their pain |
| **Body** | Explain the insight in 3-5 sentences. Make it immediately actionable. |
| **CTA** | "Hit reply if this resonates" or nothing at all |

---

## Email 3 -- Day 5: Education

**Job:** Help them think about the problem differently. Position yourself as someone who understands the space deeply.

| Field | Your Draft |
|-------|------------|
| **Subject Line** | |
| **Opening** | Frame the problem or industry context |
| **Body** | Explain why the common approach fails. Share the reframe. 3-5 sentences. |
| **CTA** | Soft -- link to a resource, article, or tool (not a sales page) |

---

## Email 4 -- Day 8: Social Proof

**Job:** Show a result. Make it specific. Numbers, before/after, or a direct quote from a customer.

| Field | Your Draft |
|-------|------------|
| **Subject Line** | |
| **Opening** | Introduce the customer or situation |
| **Body** | Short story: the problem they had, what they did, the specific result. 4-6 sentences. |
| **CTA** | "Want a result like this?" with a link to your calendar or offer page |

---

## Email 5 -- Day 12: Value + Soft CTA

**Job:** Deliver more value and make the first offer. Keep it low pressure -- a resource, not a hard close.

| Field | Your Draft |
|-------|------------|
| **Subject Line** | |
| **Opening** | Lead with value (tip, resource, or insight) |
| **Body** | 2-3 sentences of value, then 1-2 sentences introducing the offer |
| **CTA** | Soft offer: "If you'd like to explore [outcome], here's how" with link |

---

## Email 6 -- Day 16: Address Objection

**Job:** Name the most common reason people don't move forward and address it directly. This is not about convincing -- it is about removing a real barrier.

| Field | Your Draft |
|-------|------------|
| **Subject Line** | |
| **Opening** | "I hear this a lot: [objection]" |
| **Body** | Reframe the objection. Give evidence or a different perspective. 3-5 sentences. |
| **CTA** | Repeat the offer from Email 5, or offer a lower-commitment entry point |

---

## Email 7 -- Day 21: Ongoing Invitation

**Job:** Transition them to your regular cadence. Invite them into a community, weekly newsletter, or ongoing email. This is the last email in the onboarding sequence -- not the last email they ever get.

| Field | Your Draft |
|-------|------------|
| **Subject Line** | |
| **Opening** | Acknowledge they've been on this journey with you |
| **Body** | Tell them what's coming next. What they'll get from staying subscribed. 2-3 sentences. |
| **CTA** | Invite to ongoing channel: community, weekly email, social follow |

---

## Sequence Health Checklist

Before launching, verify:

- [ ] Each email has ONE job and ONE CTA
- [ ] Subject lines are plain text, not clickbait
- [ ] Emails 1-3 have zero sales language
- [ ] Email 4 uses a specific result with a number
- [ ] Email 6 names the exact objection your ICP actually says
- [ ] All emails are written in first person, conversational tone
- [ ] Sequence is loaded and tested in your email tool before going live
