# ICP Template

**Used in:** icp-definer skill
**Format:** Miro-ready -- paste as a structured sticky note or card cluster

One card per ICP segment. Complete the JTBD statement first -- it anchors everything else.

---

## ICP Card: [Segment Name]

### Jobs-to-be-Done Statement

> "When [trigger event], I want to [motivation/job], so I can [desired outcome]."

| Field | Your Answer |
|-------|-------------|
| **Trigger Event** | The specific situation that makes them look for a solution |
| **Motivation / Job** | What they are trying to accomplish in that moment |
| **Desired Outcome** | The progress they want to make -- functional or emotional |

**Completed Example:**
> "When my sales pipeline drops below 30 days of runway, I want to quickly identify which outreach sequences are converting, so I can double down on what's working before missing my number."

---

### Firmographic Profile

| Field | Your Answer |
|-------|-------------|
| **Company Size** | Number of employees (e.g., 10-50, 50-200) |
| **Industry / Vertical** | Specific, not "B2B" or "technology" |
| **Annual Revenue** | Range (e.g., $1M-$10M ARR) |
| **Stage** | Seed / Series A / bootstrapped / growth |
| **Tech Stack** | Tools they already use that signal ICP fit |
| **Geography** | Region or market if relevant |

---

### Psychographic Profile

| Field | Your Answer |
|-------|-------------|
| **Primary Pain Trigger** | The specific frustration that makes them ready to buy |
| **Secondary Pain** | The adjacent problem they also have |
| **Buying Motivation** | What outcome they are optimizing for in this purchase |
| **Risk Tolerance** | Early adopter / pragmatist / laggard |
| **Decision Maker** | Role / title of the person who signs |
| **Influencer / Champion** | Role / title of the person who finds you |
| **Top Objection** | Most common reason they don't buy or delay |

---

### Scoring

| Criterion | Score (1-5) | Rationale |
|-----------|-------------|-----------|
| **Pain Intensity** | | How urgently do they need a solution? |
| **Willingness to Pay** | | Evidence they spend on this category? |
| **Reachability** | | Can we find and reach 100 of them in 30 days? |
| **Founder Advantage** | | Network, credibility, or domain expertise here? |
| **Total** | /20 | |

**Scoring Guide:**
- 5 -- Strong evidence / direct experience
- 3 -- Some signal / reasonable assumption
- 1 -- Speculation only
