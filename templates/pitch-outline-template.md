# Pitch Outline Template

**Used in:** pitch-builder skill
**Format:** Miro-ready -- paste as a slide-by-slide card sequence

Two formats: one for Product Track (7-10 minutes) and one for Growth Track (5 minutes). Use talking points, not scripts. Keep delivery natural and founder-authentic.

---

## Product Track Pitch (7-10 minutes)

Five sections with suggested timing. Total: 7-10 minutes depending on prototype demo length.

---

### Section 1: Competitive Findings (90 seconds)

**What to cover:** What you learned about the market. Who the real competitors are. One surprising finding.

| Field | Your Talking Points |
|-------|---------------------|
| **Market context** | One sentence on the competitive landscape you found |
| **Key competitors** | 2-3 names and their primary positioning |
| **Most important finding** | The one thing that changed how you see the market |
| **How we're different** | Your positioning vs. the competitive set in one sentence |

**Transition:** "Based on what we found in the market, here's the hypothesis we chose to test..."

---

### Section 2: Hypothesis Selection (90 seconds)

**What to cover:** The hypothesis you selected and why. What you rejected and why.

| Field | Your Talking Points |
|-------|---------------------|
| **The hypothesis** | Read or paraphrase your outcome statement |
| **Why this one** | The reason this hypothesis addresses the biggest risk |
| **What you rejected** | One hypothesis you considered and why you passed |
| **Validation criteria** | What "true" looks like -- the specific metric |

**Transition:** "Here's what we built to test it..."

---

### Section 3: Prototype Demo (3-4 minutes)

**What to cover:** Show what you built. Walk through one user journey from start to finish.

| Field | Your Talking Points |
|-------|---------------------|
| **Prototype type** | What kind of prototype this is (app, landing page, script, etc.) |
| **User journey setup** | Set the scene: who is this customer and what are they trying to do? |
| **Demo walkthrough** | Live demo or walkthrough -- one complete journey |
| **Key decision point** | Where the hypothesis is tested in the demo |

**Note:** If live demo is not possible, use screenshots or a recorded walkthrough. Never apologize for the prototype -- it is supposed to be minimal.

**Transition:** "Here's what we learned..."

---

### Section 4: Key Learnings (60 seconds)

**What to cover:** What surprised you. What you would do differently. What question the prototype answered.

| Field | Your Talking Points |
|-------|---------------------|
| **What the prototype answered** | Did the hypothesis hold? Partially? Not at all? |
| **Biggest surprise** | Something you did not expect to learn |
| **What you'd change** | One thing you'd do differently in the next iteration |
| **Confidence level** | Where you are now vs. where you started this morning |

**Transition:** "Here's what we need to keep moving..."

---

### Section 5: One Ask (30 seconds)

**What to cover:** One specific ask for the cohort. Make it concrete and actionable.

| Field | Your Talking Points |
|-------|---------------------|
| **The ask** | One specific thing: an intro, a review, a resource, a challenge |
| **Why this ask** | Why this cohort is the right group to ask |
| **How to help** | Exact next step for anyone who wants to contribute |

---

## Growth Track Pitch (5 minutes)

Four sections with strict timing. Total: 5 minutes.

---

### Section 1: ICP and Offer (60 seconds)

**What to cover:** Who your beachhead customer is. What the lead magnet or offer is.

| Field | Your Talking Points |
|-------|---------------------|
| **Beachhead segment** | One sentence: who, with what trigger, wanting what outcome |
| **Lead magnet** | What you built and what it promises |
| **Offer framing** | "[Outcome] in [timeframe] using [method]" |
| **Why this segment** | Why you chose this beachhead over alternatives |

**Transition:** "Here's what we built to reach them..."

---

### Section 2: What You Built (90 seconds)

**What to cover:** The nurture sequence, automation, or content workflow you created today.

| Field | Your Talking Points |
|-------|---------------------|
| **Primary deliverable** | The main thing you built (sequence, workflow, landing page) |
| **How it works** | Walk through 2-3 steps of the sequence or automation |
| **Tool stack** | What tools you used and why (keep it simple) |
| **What's live vs. still to do** | Be honest about what is complete and what needs finishing |

**Transition:** "Here's how we're going to run this in the next 30 days..."

---

### Section 3: 30-Day Plan (90 seconds)

**What to cover:** Specific commitments with numbers. Not intentions -- commitments.

| Field | Your Talking Points |
|-------|---------------------|
| **North Star metric** | The one number you are tracking |
| **Week 1 action** | The first thing you will do on Monday |
| **30-day target** | The number you are committing to hit |
| **Decision gate** | What Green/Yellow/Red looks like for your business |

**Transition:** "Here's where we need help..."

---

### Section 4: One Ask (60 seconds)

**What to cover:** One specific ask. Be precise about what would actually move you forward.

| Field | Your Talking Points |
|-------|---------------------|
| **The ask** | One specific thing: an intro, a review, a referral, expertise |
| **Why this cohort** | Why this group is the right place to ask |
| **How to help** | The exact next step for anyone who wants to assist |

---

## Hallway Pitch (30 seconds)

For conversations after the share-out. One sentence on each.

| Field | Your Version |
|-------|--------------|
| **Who you help** | "[ICP segment] who [trigger/pain]" |
| **What you do** | "[Mechanism or product] that [outcome]" |
| **Where you are** | Stage and what you learned today |
| **The ask** | One thing you're looking for |
