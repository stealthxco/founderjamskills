# 30-Day Validation Plan Template

**Used in:** growth-builder skill, validation-planner skill
**Format:** Miro-ready -- paste as a week-by-week table or card sequence

This plan covers four weeks of validation activity with a Green/Yellow/Red decision gate at the end of Week 4. Be specific. "Do outreach" is not an action. "Send 50 cold emails to [segment] using [template] by Friday" is an action.

---

## North Star Metric

| Field | Your Answer |
|-------|-------------|
| **North Star Metric** | The single number that best measures whether this is working |
| **30-Day Target** | Specific number for Week 4 assessment |
| **Measurement Method** | How you will track this (tool, spreadsheet, manual count) |
| **Owner** | Who is responsible for hitting this number |

**North Star Metric by Business Type:**
- B2B SaaS: qualified demos booked per week
- Marketplace: transactions completed per week (both sides)
- Services: discovery calls completed per week
- Consumer app: weekly active users

### Week 4: Decide

**Goal:** Assess results against North Star target. Make a go-forward decision.

**Week 4 Decision Framework:**

| Result | Threshold | Decision |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| Green | 70%+ of North Star target | Persist -- document what's working, scale inputs |
| Yellow | 40-70% of North Star target | Optimize -- identify the one metric furthest from target and fix it before scaling |
| Red | Below 40% of North Star target | Pivot -- revisit ICP definition or offer before additional investment |
