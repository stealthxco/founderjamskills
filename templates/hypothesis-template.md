# Hypothesis Template

**Used in:** hypothesis-engine skill
**Format:** Miro-ready -- paste as a structured sticky note or table

Each hypothesis uses a dual format: an outcome statement (change-based) and a validation commitment (validation-based). Fill out both for your top hypothesis.

---

## Hypothesis Card

### Outcome Statement (Change-Based)

> "If we [change/experiment], then [target customer] will [measurable outcome] by [specific date]."

| Field | Your Answer |
|-------|-------------|
| **Change / Experiment** | What you will do or change |
| **Target Customer** | Specific segment, not "customers" |
| **Measurable Outcome** | A number: conversion rate, churn rate, revenue, usage |
| **Date** | Specific deadline, not "soon" |
| **Because (Mechanism)** | Why this change will produce this outcome |

**Completed Example:**
> "If we simplify our onboarding from 7 steps to 3, then new users will complete setup and reach their first value moment within 48 hours by [date], because friction in onboarding is the primary driver of early drop-off in our activation data."

---

### Validation Commitment (Validation-Based)

> "We believe [ICP] will pay $[Y]/month for [specific outcome]. We will validate this by [specific experiment] within [timeframe]."

| Field | Your Answer |
|-------|-------------|
| **ICP** | Specific segment with job-to-be-done trigger |
| **Price Point** | Dollar amount per month (or per transaction) |
| **Specific Outcome** | What the customer is paying for -- not features, outcomes |
| **Experiment** | How you will test this (interviews, landing page, pre-sales) |
| **Timeframe** | Days or weeks -- not "when we get to it" |

**Completed Example:**
> "We believe early-stage B2B SaaS founders will pay $199/month for a 30% reduction in time spent on manual onboarding. We will validate this by running 20 discovery calls and asking for a verbal commitment within 2 weeks."

## Hypothesis Scoring

Score each hypothesis before selecting which to test. Higher total = higher priority.

| Criterion | Score (1-5) | Rationale |
|-----------|-------------|-----------|
| **Desirability** | | Do customers actually want this outcome? |
| **Feasibility** | | Can we test this in 2 weeks with current resources? |
| **Viability** | | If true, does this meaningfully improve the business? |
| **Total** | /15 | |

**Scoring Guide:**
- 5 -- Strong evidence / high confidence
- 3 -- Some evidence / moderate confidence
- 1 -- Assumption only / low confidence
