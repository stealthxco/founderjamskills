# Founder Setup - Instructions for Claude Desktop / Cowork

## What This Skill Does

Captures your startup context and prepares you for a productive Founder Jam session. It asks about your startup, recommends a track (Product or Growth), shares quick AI tips, and creates a snapshot you can reference throughout the day.

**Time:** 15-20 minutes

## How to Use

Copy everything below the line and paste it into a new Claude Desktop or Cowork conversation.

---

## Activation Prompt

```
I'm at Founder Jam, a full-day startup workshop with two tracks: Product (competitive analysis, hypothesis, prototype) and Growth (ICP, offer design, go-to-market). I need you to help me capture my startup context before we start.

Walk me through these questions one at a time:

1. What's my startup called?
2. In one sentence, what problem does it solve?
3. Who has this problem? (Be specific: job title, company size, industry, demographic)
4. Where am I right now? (Idea stage / Pre-revenue / Early-revenue)
5. How many people on the team?
6. What traction do I have? (Users, revenue, waitlist, LOIs, anything)
7. What type of business? (B2B SaaS / Services / Marketplace / Consumer)

After collecting my answers:
- Recommend Product or Growth track based on my stage and needs
- Share 3 quick tips for getting good AI outputs during the workshop
- Create a "Startup Snapshot" summary with my details and today's activity sequence
- Optionally, offer a quick problem-solution fit score (specificity, market size, founder-problem fit, each 1-5)

Challenge my assumptions along the way:
- "Is the problem painful enough that someone would pay today to solve it?"
- "Who has this problem the worst? Are you building for them?"
- "What have you already tried to validate this?"
```

## What to Expect

1. Claude will ask you each question one at a time
2. Answer conversationally -- the more detail you share, the better your outputs will be all day
3. You'll get a track recommendation with rationale
4. Three AI tips for getting better workshop outputs
5. A startup snapshot to reference throughout the day
6. Optional: problem-solution fit score card

## Output

- Startup snapshot (save this -- other skills will ask for these details)
- Track recommendation
- 3 focus areas for the day
- Optional fit score card (paste into Miro)
